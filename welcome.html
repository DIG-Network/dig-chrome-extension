Welcome to the DIG Network Extension

You can now open chia:// links and browse DIG content right in your browser — every page is verified on-chain and decrypted on your device.

Try it

  1. Type a chia:// address straight into your browser's address bar, or
  2. Open a new tab to browse the DIG Home app directory.
chia://urn:dig:chia:<storeId>/index.html
